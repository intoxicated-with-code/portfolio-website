Hi!

I’m Paulina, visual designer & web developer with the background in finance industry. Creative since always at some point in my life I decided to give more voice to the analytical side of my brain. I got a degree in Economics and started my career in banking. However, something was missing.

First, I picked up drawing from which I resigned many years ago. Then, I got interested in different forms of design. I finished UX design course and from this moment my tech journey really began. Creating useful and beautiful content required many skills, including graphic design and converting it into a real thing by writing a code.

Months of hard work paid off. Now I’ve made a full switch in my career and am focusing on writing beautiful websites that show character of its owner.
